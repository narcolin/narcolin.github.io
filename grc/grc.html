LAES Expressive Technology Studio Logo


The new Frost Center was opened on the Cal Poly campus in Spring 2023. My academic department chairs selected the initial logo design I created for the Expressive Technology Studio in the Frost Center.

This space serves as an intersection of art, humanities and technology and home to Liberal Arts and Engineering Studies (LAES) students. The studio features a space for audio, visual and performing art production in addition to a theater art area and a black box space.

My logo was created in Adobe Illustrator. Completion process involved multiple iterations with feeback from the department chairs. Multiple versions of the logo were created for different areas in the studio (ie: door entrances, large back wall, etc).
