Digital Art

Here are some digtal art pieces I have created, using Adobe Creative Studios.


Caricature Re-Designs

One of the things I love to do is re-designing logos and caricatures. I have done re-designs of mascots, like the Salesforce MuleSoft mascot named, Max the Mule. I created a new version of Max in a SF Giants baseball uniform. I also re-created an Animal Crossing video game avatar of my sister. She likes this game and I printed out my illustration for her birthday. Both pieces below were created on Adobe Illustrator.


Personalized Artwork

I really enjoy expressing my gratitude for family and friends through personal artwork. My dad is a baseball card collector, so I thought it was a great idea to gift him his own baseball card. Some of my friends enjoy my cartoon-y and bright designs. I made a few illustrations with their favorite foods and flowers, which makes for the perfect wall art. These pieces were made on Adobe Fresco (previously Adobe Sketch).
